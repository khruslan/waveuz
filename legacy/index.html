Answers to the things every serious client asks.

Are you accredited by IT Park, AIFC, and Astana Hub?+
Yes. WaveLabs holds official resident status in IT Park Uzbekistan, AIFC (Astana International Financial Centre), and Astana Hub. Our clients benefit from internationally-recognised business infrastructure, English common law jurisdiction (AIFC), and preferred tax regimes across Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan.
How long does a typical project take?+
Most AI/ML projects run 6–16 weeks depending on data availability and scope. We give you a concrete timeline in week one, not a vague estimate.
Do you work with companies outside Uzbekistan?+
Yes. About 40% of our clients are based outside Central Asia. We work remotely across Europe, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia.
What's your minimum project size?+
We work best on projects with budgets of $15,000+. Below that, the discovery phase alone doesn't leave room to do quality work.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?+
All projects include 60 days of post-launch support. For AI systems, we offer monthly retainer packages for monitoring and retraining.
Can you work with our existing development team?+
Absolutely. Many clients bring us in as a specialist AI/ML layer on top of their internal team.
